Cookies Policy Last Updated: 25 February 2026

Welcome to Funrix Furniture. This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a smooth and personalized browsing experience.

1

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and browsing behavior, allowing for a faster and more personalized experience on return visits.

Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information on their own — they simply help your browser communicate with our website more effectively.

2

How Funrix Furniture Uses Cookies

We use cookies to deliver a better, faster, and more secure browsing experience. Specifically, we use them to:

  • Ensure core website functionality and security
  • Improve website speed and overall performance
  • Understand how visitors navigate and interact with our pages
  • Remember your preferences across sessions
  • Enhance your overall shopping and browsing experience
  • Deliver relevant content and promotions (only with your consent)
3

Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize our cookies into three types based on their purpose:

⚙️

Essential Cookies

Required for core website operations such as page navigation, security, and form submissions. Cannot be disabled.

📊

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand traffic patterns and how users interact with our website, so we can continuously improve.

🎨

Preference Cookies

Store your personal settings and preferences to ensure a more consistent and tailored experience each visit.

4

Managing Cookies

You have full control over cookies. You can configure your browser to block, delete, or restrict cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Please note that disabling certain cookies — especially essential ones — may impact the performance, functionality, or availability of certain features on our website.

🌐 Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy → Cookies
🦊 Mozilla Firefox — Options → Privacy & Security
🧭 Safari — Preferences → Privacy
🔷 Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies & Site Permissions
5

Third-Party Cookies

Some pages on our website may include content or tools from trusted third-party services (such as Google Analytics or social media plugins). These third-party services may set their own cookies on your device.

We do not control third-party cookies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with on our website.

6

Policy Updates

Funrix Furniture may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our business practices. Any revisions will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
